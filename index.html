Our Core Purpose

Combatting Pollution by Valorizing Waste

Every harvest season, millions of tonnes of agricultural stubble are burned in fields, releasing severe pollutants and greenhouse gases into the atmosphere. This practice degrades soil health and creates a public health crisis.

Sunita Biofuels was established to break this destructive cycle. We view crop residue not as waste, but as a valuable energy resource. By creating a market for this residue, we incentivize farmers against burning, improve air quality, and provide industries with a cleaner fuel alternative.

Advanced Densification Technology

Raw biomass is bulky and inefficient to transport or burn. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility uses high-pressure densification technology to compress this material. The result is a uniform, dense pellet with significantly reduced moisture content and a much higher calorific value by volume.

Efficient, Cleaner Combustion

Sunita Biofuels pellets are designed for direct co-firing in existing coal boilers. They burn consistently and efficiently, emitting significantly lower levels of sulfur oxides (SOx) and nitrogen oxides (NOx) compared to coal, helping industries meet stringent environmental regulations.
